General Questions
What devices is Mobigeek compatible with?
The MobiGeek Mobile Controller is compatible with all iPhone / Android models with 4.5-6.5 inch screens.
What games can I play using The MobiGeek Mobile Controller?
The gamepad is comparable with many shooting games, including Pubg, Fortnite, Critical Ops, Call of Duty Mobile, Hitman sniper, Rules of Survival, Knives Out, Survivor Royale, Creative Destruction, Garena Free Fire, Modern Combat 5 and many other games, which allow you to customize the location of the buttons.
How to charge the battery?
Please use a USB cable to charge when the battery of the game controller is running low. It means normal charging status when the power indicator of the game controller is blinking; similarly, the indicators will be long bright once the battery has been charged completely, USB cable could be removed.
How to set up the game to match the controller?
  • Enter the game main interface, and click the "setting" button;
  • Enter the "setting panel", choose "controller setting" and then click "custom panel"
  • Enlarge the "shooting" button size and move to the top left of the screen, move the "aim" key to the top right of the screen or adjust as a personal preference;
  • Install the Mobile PUBG Game Controller, adjust the conductive silicone head to the "shooting" and "aim" key;
  • Press the metal trigger can achieve shooting;
  • When the phone is overheated, press the switch button on the back side;
  • Please use the matching charging cable to charge cellphone when needed. One end is for the phone, the other end-USB port is for the output port of the mobile controller.
